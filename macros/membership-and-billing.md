# Membership & Billing Macros

## 1. Billing Confusion – First Response

> Hi {customer_name},  
> Thanks for getting in touch about this – I’m glad you reached out.
>
> I’ve taken a look at your account and the charge for **{amount}** on **{date}** relates to **{brief explanation if known}**.
>
> I know billing can be confusing, so let me break it down clearly:
> - **{Point 1 – what the charge is for}**  
> - **{Point 2 – time period or product}**  
> - **{Point 3 – any discounts/taxes applied}**
>
> If anything still doesn’t look right after this, let me know which part doesn’t match what you expected and I’ll dig deeper.
