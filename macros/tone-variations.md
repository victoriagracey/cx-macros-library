## ðŸ§  Notes on Choosing the Right Tone
- **Formal:** escalations, sensitive topics, or older audiences  
- **Warm:** supportive, relationship-building situations  
- **Concise:** busy customers, repeated questions  
- **Empathetic:** emotional, stressful, or complex issues  
- **Friendly:** social media, brand personality  
- **Assertive:** policy misunderstandings or repeated incorrect assumptions  

Tone should adapt to:
- customer preference  
- brand voice  
- severity of the situation  
- communication channel
